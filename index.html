About Me

Hello, my name is I Gede Kasuma Dana, though most people call me Kasuma. I'm a student at SMK Negeri 1 Denpasar majoring in Software Development (RPL) and a graduate of the Coding Camp by DBS Foundation 2025, where I participated in the Front-end Back-end learning path. Born and raised in this vibrant city, I have always been passionate about technology, and I also enjoy photography, capturing life's interesting moments as a hobby.

One of my most memorable experiences occurred when I was in the 2nd grade. I had the rare opportunity to perform at the Bali Arts Festival. Stepping onto the stage at the Art Center in Ardha Candra with my art group, Sanggar Drupadi, and the music ensemble Palawara opened my eyes to the beauty of the arts and ignited a creative spark that I continue to nurture. Conversely, the passing of my great-grandparents was a profoundly sorrowful event, especially losing my great-grandfather, a cherished figure who always accompanied me as I played musical instruments in our village. This experience taught me the importance of cherishing every moment with our loved ones.

Throughout my educational journey, I actively participated in the Spensix Multimedia Computer Club, where I honed my skills in Microsoft Word. During this time, I achieved several accolades, such as winning competitions at Alfa Prima and a local high school event, earning recognition in a quiz contest organized by FAD Denpasar, and excelling in an essay contest held by Bank BPD Bali. In the 8th grade, I was entrusted with the role of Microsoft Word coordinator and later served as the Vice Chairman of the club. Without this involvement, I might never have developed the interest in software engineering that has defined my educational path.

Looking ahead, I aspire to continue developing my potential. After completing my vocational education, I plan to further my studies in Informatics Engineering at Politeknik Negeri Bali, while also pursuing part-time work to gain additional experience in the ever-evolving world of technology.
